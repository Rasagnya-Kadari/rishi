Rishi MS Institute of Engineering & Technology for women was established in 2009 to perpetuate the memory of “ BHARAT RATNA”. Smt. M.S. Subbulakshmiji to emulate moral values and quality parameters in Technical Education. Rishi MS Engineering College is located near JNTUH metro station, KUKATPALLY, HYDERABAD.

The college has a strong TRAINING & PLACEMENT CELL, with continuous activities to ensure best Technical and Soft skills to students for industry readiness. The students from all the branches are participating in INTERNSHIPS, HACKATHON, WORKSHOPS, and skill development workshops in updated Technologies from time to time. The college had MOU with CISCO and TASK is the best platform for updating skills with multi-level technical training programs. Placement cell has achieved 100% placements for IV year students in reputed companies.
